Greeting in English

Visit Home

Every non-native speaker needs English conversation practice, but oftentimes the hardest part of it is getting started.

There are many ways to say hello in English. Sometimes you say a quick hello as you are passing somebody. At other times a greeting leads to a conversation. Friends and family members greet each other in a casual way. Business greetings are more formal.

On the following pages you can listen to and practise greeting people in a variety of situations.

You will only need to learn a few common greetings, but the words you use will vary based on the situation. For example, in a job interview, you should use a formal greeting, but when you meet a friend for coffee, you’ll likely say something informal.

Formal Greetings

Hello
Good morning
Good afternoon
Good evening
It’s nice to meet you
It’s a pleasure to meet you

Informal Greetings

Hi
Hey
What’s up
Nice to meet you
Pleased to meet you
It’s a pleasure
